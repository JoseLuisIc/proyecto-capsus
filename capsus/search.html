Ministry of Trade and Industry - Department of Statistics / 11 Nov 2020

Malay-Indian, Malay-Chinese, Indian-Others Total may include marriages with attribute(s) not reported.

First Marriages for Couples under the Women's Charter by Educational Qualification of Brides and Grooms, Annual

Ministry of Trade and Industry - Department of Statistics / 11 Nov 2020

Statistics on marriages registered under the Women’s Charter are obtained from records maintained by the Registry of Marriages. They include all civil marriages solemnized by the Registrar of Marriages, pastors of churches, priests of Hindu temples and other registered solemnizers. Data pertain to couples where neither party had previously been married. Total may include marriages with attribute(s) not reported.

Exports Of Services By Major Trading Partner, Annual

Domestic Wholesale Trade Index, (2017 = 100), Current, Quarterly, (SA)

Ministry of Trade and Industry - Department of Statistics / 11 Nov 2020

The Wholesale Trade Index (WTI) measures the short-term performance of wholesale trade activities. Data for the compilation of the WTI are sourced from over 700 wholesale establishments covered in the quarterly survey of wholesale trade as well as from administrative records. The indices are presented at both current prices and constant prices. The indices at current prices measure the changes in sales values which can result from changes in both price and quantity. By removing the price effect, the indices at constant prices measure the changes in sales volume. The base year for the WTI is 2017. The Domestic WTI records wholesale sales in Singapore.

Business Expectations For The Services Sector - Employment Forecast For The Next Quarter, Weighted Percentages Of Up, Same, Down, Quarterly

Ministry of Trade and Industry - Department of Statistics / 11 Nov 2020

The data are compiled from the results of the quarterly Business Expectations Survey of the Services Sector. The objective of the survey is to obtain the business outlook for the immediate future of firms in the services sector. The firms’ views are expressed in terms of directional change(i.e. "up", "same" or "down"). These responses are then weighted and aggregated to derive the weighted percentages for "up", "same" or "down" for each industry. The net weighted balance, which is calculated by the differences between the weighted percentages of "ups" and "downs" is used to indicated the likely overall direction of change of a particular industry.

Availability of Selected Consumer Durables/Service, Quinquennial

Ministry of Trade and Industry - Department of Statistics / 11 Nov 2020

The Singapore Department of Statistics undertakes the Household Expenditure Survey (HES) once in 5 years to collect detailed information from resident households in Singapore. Availability of consumer durables includes items available for use by households irrespective of whether the items are rented, owned by households or obtained from other sources, e.g. car provided by the company. Items available only for work purposes are excluded. Items must be in working condition to be considered.

Annulments Under The Women's Charter By Ethnic Group Of Couple, Annual

Ministry of Trade and Industry - Department of Statistics / 11 Nov 2020

Annulments granted under the Women's Charter refer to invalidation of a marriage. Parties whose marriages have been annulled are conferred the status of never having been married to each other. Statistics on annulments under the Women's Charter are obtained from records maintained by the Family Justice Courts. Total may include annulments with attribute(s) not reported.
